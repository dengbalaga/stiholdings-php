Welcome to STI Education Systems Holdings Inc.

STI Education Systems Holdings, Inc. (PSE Code: “STI”) is a leading education and investments corporation in the Philippines. STI Holdings is recognized as one of the largest network of schools in the country today with a total number of 80,200  students enrolled in 80 STI-branded schools nationwide composed of 66-STI branded colleges and 14-STI branded educational centers, two non-branded colleges, and one university.

In 2012, STI Holdings posted yet another milestone as it listed follow-on shares in the Philippine Stock Exchange, making it 30 percent publicly owned. The listing infused about P2.36 billion in fresh capital for the company.
